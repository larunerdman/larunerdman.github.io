What we have to offer

Located on a quiet street in the centre of Toronto, our newly renovated home offers a good night's rest and place to relax during your visit. We are minutes away from vibrant neighbourhoods like Kensington Market, Chinatown, Yonge and Dundas Square, and sites like the Art Gallery of Ontario and CN Tower. This is the perfect place to enjoy all that Toronto has to offer!

The neighbourhood

We're located on a quiet street in the Kensington Market-Chinatown neighbourhood of Toronto, one of the most colourful and diverse places you'll ever visit. Walk for five minutes in any direction and you'll find great shops, cafes, and restaurants serving food from all around the world. We're conveniently located a three minute walk from the closest streetcar stop and an eight minute walk away from the nearest subway station. This neighbourhood is very safe at all times of day (and night).

What makes us different

Location, breakfast included, hosts are local Torontonians who have worked as city tour guides and can give lots of great recommendations of places to visit, restaurants to try, and activities for people of all ages. We can also provide you with discount coupons to visit a number of local attractions (CN Tower, the ROM, etc.) and nearby restaurants.
